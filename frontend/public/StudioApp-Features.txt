STUDIOAPP — FEATURE LIST (Photographer & Client)
For website / marketing use
====================================================================


--------------------------------------------------------------------
FOR PHOTOGRAPHERS (YOUR STUDIO ACCOUNT)
--------------------------------------------------------------------

GETTING STARTED
• Create your own studio account and start on a free trial
• Simple, tiered plans — upgrade any time as you grow
• Clean, modern admin dashboard to manage everything in one place
• Built-in Help Centre with searchable step-by-step guides

YOUR BRANDING
• Upload your own studio logo
• Set your studio name, tagline and contact email
• Choose your accent colour to match your brand
• Your branding appears across galleries, client pages and emails
• Branded share links (e.g. yourdomain/s/your-studio/couple-name)

GALLERIES
• Create unlimited client galleries (within your plan)
• Organise each gallery into multiple folders / sub-galleries
• Save reusable folder templates to set up new galleries in seconds
• Set a cover image for galleries and folders
• Rename, reorganise and manage galleries with ease
• Attach a client email address to each gallery

UPLOADS
• Upload thousands of photos in a single drop (1,000+ supported)
• Smart batched uploading for fast, reliable large transfers
• Live upload progress with clear success/failure feedback
• Upload high-resolution images and video files
• Automatic thumbnail generation for fast browsing
• Copy or move images between folders

VIDEO
• Upload your films straight into a gallery
• Automatic web-optimised versions created for smooth online playback
• Hardware-accelerated processing for high-quality, fast delivery
• Videos stream reliably to clients on any device

SHARING WITH CLIENTS
• Generate secure, branded share links for each gallery
• Optional password protection on any share
• Optional expiry dates to keep galleries time-limited
• Control access levels — view-only or full download
• Choose whether clients can download or delete
• Custom, memorable link names (e.g. the couple's names)
• Instant QR codes + downloadable QR code PDFs for prints/albums
• Optional guest-upload mode so guests can add their own photos

CLIENT EMAILS & AUTOMATION
• Connect your own sending email (SMTP) to email from your address
• Send a "your gallery is ready" notification with the link included
• Reusable email templates for consistent, on-brand messaging
• Broadcast a single message to all your clients at once
• AUTOMATIC expiry reminders — clients are emailed 7 days before a
  gallery closes, prompting them to download everything in good time
  (sent once per gallery, signed with your studio name)

PRINT SALES
• Let clients order prints directly from their gallery
• Receive and manage incoming print orders

INSIGHTS & CONTROL
• Activity tracking so you can see engagement on your galleries
• Plan usage meter showing galleries used vs. your allowance
• Full control to delete galleries or individual images
• Everything kept private and separated per studio


--------------------------------------------------------------------
FOR YOUR CLIENTS (COUPLES & GUESTS)
--------------------------------------------------------------------

EFFORTLESS ACCESS
• Open their gallery from a simple, branded link — no account needed
• Works beautifully on phones, tablets and desktops
• Secure password entry when the photographer enables it
• Scan a QR code to jump straight to their gallery

VIEWING
• Browse the gallery by folders / moments
• Fast, progressive image loading for a smooth experience
• Full-screen lightbox viewing of every image
• Elegant full-screen slideshow mode to relive the day

VIDEO STREAMING
• Stream their wedding/film videos online, right in the gallery
• Smooth, buffer-friendly playback on any device — no downloads needed

DOWNLOADS & FAVOURITES
• Download high-resolution images (when enabled by the photographer)
• Mark favourite photos to shortlist their best shots
• Submit their favourites selection back to the photographer

EXTRAS
• Order prints directly from their gallery (when enabled)
• Upload their own guest photos when guest-upload mode is on
• Friendly automatic reminder email before the gallery expires, so
  they never miss the chance to save their memories
