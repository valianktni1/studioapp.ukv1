How To Choose Your Favourites

Your Simple Guide to Selecting Album Photos

Congratulations on your wedding! Your gallery is now ready for you to browse. This guide will show you how to select your favourite photos for your album. Take your time, enjoy reliving the memories, and don't worry - you can change your selections at any time, up until you have submitted your favourites to me for album design.

1

Browse Your Gallery

Click on any album folder (e.g., "Wedding Images", "Ceremony", "Reception") to view your photos. You can click on any photo to see it larger, and use the arrows to move between images.

Tip: Take your time! There's no rush. You can come back as many times as you like.
2

Heart Your Favourites

When you see a photo you love, simply tap the heart icon on the image. The heart will turn gold to show it's been added to your favourites. To remove a photo from your favourites, just tap the heart again.

Tip: You can favourite photos from the thumbnail view or when viewing a photo full-screen.
3

Review Your Selections

Click the "Favourites" button at the top of the page to see all the photos you've selected. This lets you review your choices in one place. You'll see a count showing how many photos you've chosen.

Tip: Your favourites are automatically saved - even if you close the browser and come back later!
4

Submit for Your Album

Once you're happy with your selections, click the "Submit for Album" button. Your chosen photos will be sent to me, and I'll use them to create your beautiful wedding album.

Tip: You can submit your favourites multiple times if you change your mind - I'll always use your most recent selection.
